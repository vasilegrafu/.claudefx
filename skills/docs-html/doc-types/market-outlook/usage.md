# market-outlook

- Audience: the investor (and later graders of the call) — outlooks are
  written to be scored, not admired.
- Filename: `docs/market-outlook-<period>.html` (e.g. `-2026-h2`).
- Depth: ask.
- Rules: scenarios carry probabilities that sum to ~100%; every claim in "the
  view" maps to an indicator or an assumption in a scenario; the falsifiers
  section is mandatory and specific — an outlook nothing could disprove is
  marketing; grade last period's outlook in the new one's change-history.
