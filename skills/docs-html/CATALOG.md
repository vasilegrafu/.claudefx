- `business-case` — justification for an investment or initiative

- `incident-postmortem` — blameless analysis of an incident and follow-ups

- `service-level-agreement` — committed service levels and remedies
- `standard-operating-procedure` — step-by-step procedure for a routine task
- `statement-of-work` — contractual scope, deliverables and terms of an engagement

- `user-guide` — end-user instructions for a product
